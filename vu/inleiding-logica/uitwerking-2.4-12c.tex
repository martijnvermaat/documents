De formule $\forall x(Px \rightarrow \exists y Qy) \rightarrow \forall x \exists y(Px \rightarrow Qy)$ is
een tautologie (overtuig jezelf hiervan). We geven een afleiding:

\begin{equation*}
\begin{fitch}
\fh \forall x(Px \rightarrow \exists y Qy)     & assumptie                        \\ % 1
\fa \fitchmodal{x_0} Px_0 \vee \neg Px_0       & LEM                              \\ % 2
\fa \fa \fh Px_0                               & assumptie                        \\ % 3
\fa \fa \fa Px_0 \rightarrow \exists y Qy      & $\forall x_{\text{e}}$ 1         \\ % 4
\fa \fa \fa \exists y Qy                       & MP 4, 3                          \\ % 5
\fa \fa \fa \fitchmodalh{y_0} Qy_0             & assumptie                        \\ % 6
\fa \fa \fa \fa \fh Px_0                       & assumptie                        \\ % 7
\fa \fa \fa \fa \fa Qy_0                       & copy 6                           \\ % 8
\fa \fa \fa \fa Px_0 \rightarrow Qy_0          & $\rightarrow_{\text{i}}$ 7-8     \\ % 9
\fa \fa \fa \fa \exists y(Px_0 \rightarrow Qy) & $\exists y_{\text{i}}$ 9         \\ % 10
\fa \fa \fa \exists y(Px_0 \rightarrow Qy)     & $\exists y_{\text{e}}$ 5, 6-10   \\ % 11
\fa \fa \fh \neg Px_0                          & assumptie                        \\ % 12
\fa \fa \fa \fh Px_0                           & assumptie                        \\ % 13
\fa \fa \fa \fa \bot                           & $\neg_{\text{e}}$ 13, 12         \\ % 14
\fa \fa \fa \fa Qy_1                           & $\bot_{\text{e}}$ 14             \\ % 15
\fa \fa \fa Px_0 \rightarrow Qy_1              & $\rightarrow_{\text{i}}$ 13-15   \\ % 16
\fa \fa \fa \exists y(Px_0 \rightarrow Qy)     & $\exists y_{\text{i}}$ 16        \\ % 17
\fa \fa \exists y(Px_0 \rightarrow Qy)         & $\vee_{\text{e}}$ 2, 3-11, 12-17 \\ % 18
\fa \forall x \exists y(Px \rightarrow Qy)     & $\forall x_{\text{i}}$ 2-18      \\ % 19
\forall x(Px \rightarrow \exists y Qy) \rightarrow \forall x \exists y(Px \rightarrow Qy) & $\rightarrow_{\text{i}}$ 1-19 \\ % 20
\end{fitch}
\end{equation*}
